Hexweave Bag

This tool will calculate the total cost for a Hexweave Bag using primal spirits to bypass the daily cooldown.

Set a price for "Sumptuous Fur" and "Material".

"Sumptuous Fur" represents the Sumptous Furs needed to craft Hexweave cloth.

"Material" represents the material you use to trade for Primal Spirits, in other words: WoD herbs/ores/dust/fur and leather.
