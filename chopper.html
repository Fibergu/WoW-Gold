Mekgineer's Chopper/Mechano-Hog

This tool will calculate the total cost for a Chopper and determine the cheapest way to craft it.

If you have Mining and Alchemy: Put a price in every box.

If you don't have Alchemy: Put a price in every box except "Saronite Bar".

If you don't have Mining: Put a price in every box except "Saronite Bar" and "Titanium Bar".
